This website provides demographic information as well as visualization of a large database from a company researching bacterial species. In this study,the researcher is testing the hypothesis that there is a microorganism within the human body that could supply the next best beef taste. Researches recorded bacterial species found in human belly buttons with the purpose to try to find a bacteria that have the ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef.
